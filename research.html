Methodology & findings

Our research

A trauma-informed, human-centred approach to understanding housing and service access — designed with, not just for, the Traveller community.

Methodology

Trauma-informed, human-centred research

Given the history of discrimination and institutional harm many Traveller families have experienced, PATHS is built around methods that prioritise safety, trust, and genuine collaboration at every stage.

Safety & trust first

Sessions are designed to give participants control over what they share, with familiar facilitators, flexible pacing, and no pressure to disclose more than they're comfortable with.

Human-centred design

Workshops and interviews are structured around participants' own priorities and language, rather than a fixed research agenda imposed from outside.

Community partnership

Traveller-led organisations are involved in shaping the research design, recruiting participants, and reviewing findings before they're published.
